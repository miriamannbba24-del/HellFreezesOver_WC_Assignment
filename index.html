✦ Our Story

Born from a dare,
perfected with love.

Hell Freezes Over began as a bet between two friends who believed the best ice cream in the world could be made right here, with local farms, real fruit, and old-fashioned churning techniques passed down through generations.

Every scoop we serve is made fresh daily. We source our dairy from family farms, hand-pick seasonal fruits, and never — ever — use artificial colors, flavors, or preservatives. Because real food tastes better. Full stop.

We specialize in single-origin ice creams, inventive popsicles, and show-stopping frozen desserts that turn occasions into memories.
